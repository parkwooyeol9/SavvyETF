Daily automation

Schedule (KST)

All times are Korea Standard Time. Schedulers wait 3 minutes after boot before running.

Boot + 3 min

Cache warmup

ETF rankings load first, then S&P 500 and NASDAQ 100 in the background. A watchdog refreshes stale caches every 10 minutes.

Daily 17:00

Macro dashboard

Same as /macro — charts and AI macro risk comment broadcast to registered chats.

After US close + ~5 min

Post-close market brief

Waits for SPY daily bar on Yahoo Finance, then sends /summary. Roughly 05:05–06:05 KST depending on US daylight saving.

Daily 22:00

Fixed evening brief

Second /summary run with refreshed rankings, heatmap, and AI briefing. Saved to /summary.

Under the hood

Built for 24/7 on Render Starter (512MB)

Memory safety

  • Heavy tasks serialized (summary, macro, cache warmup)
  • Playwright runs in short-lived child processes
  • 180s startup grace before schedulers fire

Cache strategy

  • ETF warmed at boot; S&P/NAS deferred
  • Stale on-disk cache used if fresh TTL (1h) expired
  • On-demand warmup when you run /sp or /summary

Telegram delivery

  • Broadcasts to TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID + known chats
  • Auto-blocks chats that return 400/403

Ranking modes

surge — price up × volume ratio
dropvol — |price down| × volume ratio
Default shows top 3 from each pattern (6 tickers).
